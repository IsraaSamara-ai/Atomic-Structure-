Overview
An interactive educational web application that explains atomic structure, Bohr's model, quantum physics concepts, X-rays, and laser technology with 3D animations, real-time calculations, and bilingual content (Arabic/English).

Features
🌐 Interactive 3D Bohr atom model with electron transitions
📊 Real-time photon energy, wavelength, and frequency calculators
🎨 Emission & absorption spectrum visualizations
🔬 Wave-particle duality with diffraction pattern simulation
📐 De Broglie wavelength calculator with derivation
🏥 X-ray tube visualization and spectrum analysis
⚡ Laser technology and applications
🔊 Sound effects for electron transitions
📱 Fully responsive design
Topics Covered
Bohr's Postulates for Hydrogen Atom
Energy Levels & Mathematical Equations
Photon Energy Calculations (Emission/Absorption)
Atomic Spectra (Emission Line, Absorption Line, Continuous)
Hydrogen Spectrum & Rydberg Formula
Wave-Particle Duality of Radiation & Matter
De Broglie Hypothesis & Derivation
X-Rays in Physics & Medicine
Technological Applications (X-rays, Laser)
